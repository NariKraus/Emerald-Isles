Theocracy of Sorasene

Coast, Desert, Swamp

While they are governed by a theocracy, Sorasene doesn't follow any single deity, but rather each of the 20 deities, good, evil and all in between, have a respective high priest whose role it is to negotiate and decide upon any rulings with all the other high priests. These meetings will typically be heavily guarded against both internal and external threats, as the high priests are skilled mages, some of whom are more than willing to make their point with force.

Sorasene is blessed with beautiful deserts and marshlands, only making room for true woodland on their borders with Syran. Because of this, the natives of Sorasene are extremophiles and are rarely seen away from the island's shores. That being said, there are stories of Thri-Kreen mages who trained within the borders of Bothamia, and Khenra pirates who sail the ocean in search of new bounties.
