Kingdom of Bothamia

Forest, Mountain

The Kingdom of Bothamia is well renowned for its prestigious mystical colleges and abundance of arcane energy. It is common knowledge that the density of magic within the Kingdom can both help and hinder the casting of spells, so those with magical talents do well to practice within the protective shadow of The Bronze Highland.

Bothamia is ruled over by a royal family, led by the current reigning Monarch, King Nayan Redwind. The Redwind family consists of some of the most powerful mages in the Emerald Isles, having mastered multiple forms of magic and utilizing it to fairly defend their honour and titles against challengers.

The Bronze Highland is the tallest mountain within the Emerald Isles, earning its name through its ability to withstand the savage effects of time and terror alike. Legends speak of a Great Wyrm that lays dormant near the peak, or ancient artefacts capable of empowering even the lowliest mage to a state of pseudo immortality or blades with the power to slay the gods themselves.

Upon The Bronze Highland lay many towns and cities, many of which are home to incredible colleges of magic. Resting almost at the peak of the mountain lays the Capital, Skallegus, resting over 6500 meters above sea level. Skallegus not only boasts the highest population of all the burgs in the Emerald Isles, but also the greatest percentage of able mages.
