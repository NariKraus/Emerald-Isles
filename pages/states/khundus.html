Principality of Khundus

Coast, Forest, Mountain

The Principality of Khundus is a state whose traditions have stood the tests of time for centuries. The Principality was originally a Kingdom ruled over by the Dwarves, however after a plague struck down many of the inhabitants of the state including the King, his next of kin decided to honour her father and name the state as a Principality, allowing their father to be the last King of Khundus, taken before his time.

The plague wiped out many of the Dwarves on Khundus, but the more resilient Lizardfolk and Dragonborn minorities were left more or less unscathed. Because of this opportunity, the Dragonborn population quickly began to grow and overtake the Dwarves to become the dominant race in the state. The Dragonborn did however respect their predecessor's laws and culture, allowing the Princess to maintain control.

Many generations have passed since the dwarfish plague, but their culture still stands strong, and to this day, there still remains a dwarfish Prince or Princess on the throne.
