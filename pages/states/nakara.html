Nakaran Kingdom

Coast, Grassland

The Nakaran Kingdom lies on a small group of islands under Drow control. Due to the stretched nature of the islands, the kingdom has many coastal burgs which, when combined with the Drow's distrustful nature, has led to the production of the Aurora Spirit Fleet. The Aurora Spirit Fleet is by far the greatest naval force within the Emerald Isles.

Despite being a direct branch off of the elven family tree, the Drow tend to be most active at night due to their sensitive eyes, and will often build their major cities to be partially subterranean, using caverns in cliff faces and creating giant mystical light sources that are less harmful to their vision.

Despite their King's caution of other nations, Nakara is one of the biggest cultivators and traders of crops. Their magical lights excel at enhancing the growth and quality of crops to the point where they export a large majority of their produce to other states.
