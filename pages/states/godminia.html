Godminian Empire

Coast, Forest, Grassland, Mountain

The Godminian Empire is the largest state in the Emerald Isles, dwarfing even Sorasene in both population and territory. The majority of Godminia's inhabitants are of Human or Dwarfish origin, however most of the races that thrive within the Emerald Isles can be found within the two major landmasses.

The western side of the Roaring Mountains, Godminia's major mountain line, is home to many tribes of Goblinoids and Kobolds who frequently get into combat and disputes over one another's land and territory. While they appear threatening, the Goblins will tend to allow travellers and locals to pass through their territories freely and will very rarely attack anyone unprovoked. Similarly, the Kobold groups will offer protection to those who are willing to gift them with foods or resources.

Despite their size, the Godminian Empire has only a small military force. Seen as they are a non-judgemental and equal state, most other states have little quarrel with them, meaning trade throughout the Emerald Isles typically localises around Godminia's Capital. Even the distrustful Drow and the War-Ready Yuan-ti have strong diplomatic connections to the Emperor of Godminia.
