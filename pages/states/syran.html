Divine Kingdom of Syran

Coast, Forest, Grassland

Home to Fey of all shapes and sizes, Syran is an enchanting state filled with mysteries and curiosities. While pixies play pranks on one another, and fairies flutter through the skies, Dryads lead their Treant groves on their migratory paths and Satyrs revel in song and dance.

Making their homes among the golden beaches and majestic forests, Aarakocra, Aven and Sirens are a common sight, gliding graciously through the clouds. The Verdan have also made this land their home, growing beautiful orchards and thriving off of the lands. They helped build the bridges that connected the Civilised with the Wild and allowed them to co-exist peacfully beside one another.
