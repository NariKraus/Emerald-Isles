The Crystal Crown

The Crystal Crown is an order of Paladins based within Khundus. Though small in number, they hold great influencial power and are known to make a large impact on the lives of those who are loyal to Bahamut.

The order is comprised almost entirely out of Dragonborn, though one of the 3 commanders is known to be a half-dragon of red ansestry who shunned the evil ways of their draconic parent.
