Hunters of the Silver Serpent

The Hunters of the Silver Serpent are a mysterious group of mercenaries who take their work to the skies.

After striking gold in a literal sense, the small group grew to own a small fleet of Sky Ships kept aloft though large quantities of Skybough Amber. Utilising managed explosions of gunpowder, the group discovered a way to create rudimentary yet agile flying boards, allowing a well practiced user to easily out speed even the fastest horse.

As their name suggests, the Hunters seek out Silver Dragons. Upon location, the ship will contact the Dragon in an attempt to take them to a safe location, hidden deep within the Roaring Mountains of Godminia. Through these efforts and a deal once struck with the Emperor of Godminia, the crew have been able to continue to grow their treasury.

It should be noted that, while their main intentions may seem pure of heart, the Hunters are dictated purely through coin and will engage or avoid events depending on the profitability.
