Isles of Cal'en

Forest, Swamp

The Isles of Cal'en are inhabited by Yuan-ti in all shapes and forms. Every town and city has an elected group of elites, typically Purebloods, though there have been exceptions in the past. These groups when brought together form the Council of Cal'en and dictate their laws and goals.

Many Yuan-ti are outcast from the burgs, such as the Abominations, Anathema and any Broodguard that have served their purpose or failed trying. While the Yuan-ti as a whole are separated and segregated, they do not inherently hate those within another class or social status, merely seeing it as luck-of-the-draw, and some, such as the Priests and Pit Masters have been known to raise up in status through their efforts.

The social classes of Yuan-ti are as follows:

  • Purebloods, Priests
  • Pit Masters, Nightmare Speakers, Mind Whisperers
  • Malisons
  • Abominations, Anathemas
  • Broodguard (Typically slaves or discarded)
