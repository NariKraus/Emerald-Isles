The Emerald Isles is the most beautiful archipelago the world has to offer.

With crystal clear waters and golden sands, clear skies and thousands of wild flowers. It is the perfect location for those seeking some calm with plenty of adventure on offer. Here, you can find a piece of paradise.

  • Diplomatic and cultural barriers ranging between each of the states owning land on these seas.
  • Pillaging Pirates and Bothersome Brigands plague the quiet trails that adventurers travel with blinding loyalty.
  • Beasts and demons lurk in the shadows, while pixies and fauns plot their schemes, and mountains of scales lay resting below the shore.

While the Emerald Isles may be an alluring and beautiful landscape, the ocean's calm is often worse than it's storms, and the shores are deadlier still. Monsters terrorize the wilderness, while Demons plot and Fey scheme. Only those who sport great courage and power can reach the stars and join the stories of the island's great mythos.
